Have you ever bought a video game, played it for a week and then never even touched it again? If so, you’re not the only one. Video games get boring when they are too repetitive with little to no variation
are predictable and offer no surprises to the player, don't present enough of a
challenge for players, have poor pacing that requires too much work for too little
reward, or simply aren't fun from the beginning
